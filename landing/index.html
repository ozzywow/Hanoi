Help & FAQ

How do I get on the online ranking?

Set a personal best on any level and it's submitted automatically. Tap the ranking board to browse each level's world leaderboard.

My new record isn't showing on the board

Rankings can take a few seconds to propagate on the server. Reopen the ranking board after a moment — your record is safely stored and will appear.

What is Ghost Race?

You race side by side against a top player's recorded run. Beat their time to leave your mark; if you're beaten, a Revenge challenge lets you try again.

How do I change the language?

The game follows your device language automatically and supports many languages out of the box.

Is the game free?

Yes. Some higher levels can be unlocked via an optional in-app purchase.
